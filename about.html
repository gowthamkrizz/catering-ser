Founded 2004

From a Small Kitchen to a Legacy

Stackly was born in 2004 when founder Arjun Mehta, a culinary graduate with a dream, began catering small family functions from his mother's kitchen in Mumbai. What started as a passion project quickly grew into a word-of-mouth phenomenon.

By 2010, Stackly had catered its 500th event and expanded its team to 30 chefs. Today, with over 1,000 events completed and a team of 50+ culinary professionals, we are one of Maharashtra's most trusted premium catering brands.

Our commitment has never wavered ? to deliver food that sparks joy, creates memories, and tells the story of every occasion we're honoured to be a part of.

Quality First

Our Sourcing Standards

We believe that an extraordinary dish begins with exceptional ingredients. Our kitchen adheres to the highest standards of culinary sourcing:

Farm-to-Table Freshness

Vegetables, herbs, and dairy products are sourced fresh every single morning directly from local cooperative farms.

FSSAI Certified Sourcing

All grain and dry goods are sourced only from certified organic suppliers who meet premium safety standards.

Exotic Spices & Blends

Our garam masalas and spice blends are ground in-house using whole spices sourced from their native regions in India.
